About

I am a senior in the BFA program for Digital Art and Animation at Lousiana State University. I became passionate about art at an early age, and I realized that it is what I want to do with my life when I was about 14. I enjoy all types of art and creation, but my ideal career is one involved in the car-design process. My dad got me into cars when I was young and I became interested in motorcycles as well after watching the 2010's Tron: Legacy. I became a Christian my freshman year of college and as such I believe that I should work as if I were working for God in all that I do, and in that I aim to do my best in any work that I have before me. I love cars, motorcycles, creating art, sports, rock climbing, reading, and Jesus.
